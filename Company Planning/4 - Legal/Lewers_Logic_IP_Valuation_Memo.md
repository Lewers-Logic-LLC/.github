## Valuation Methodology

**Cost Approach (Floor):**
- Recycler.IQ: $126,000
- Legal Mind: $12,600
- Represents sunk development costs; establishes defensive minimum

**Income Approach:**
- Projects 3-year revenue based on market opportunity and customer interest
- Applies risk discounts (20–60%) reflecting pre-revenue status and execution risk
- Further reduces for incompleteness and market validation uncertainty

**Why Two Valuations:**
- **Conservative:** Defensible against IRS challenge; assumes lower success probability
- **Market-Realistic:** Reflects actual value if challenged; assumes moderate success based on partnerships and customer interest
