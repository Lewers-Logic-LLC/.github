## 3. Asset Descriptions

### 3.1 Recycler.IQ

Recycler.IQ is a multi-tenant SaaS platform for waste management and recycling operations, currently 40% complete in development. To date, 1,000 hours of software development have been invested at $125/hour, totaling $125,500 in labor, plus $500 in infrastructure costs, for a cumulative development investment of $126,000.

The platform provides operational management and monitoring capabilities for recycler fleet operations, with particular focus on the cash recycler segment. Recycler.IQ serves two primary customer segments: (1) tax authority customers operating cash recycling operations, and (2) retail operators managing recycler fleets.

**Market Traction:** Recycler.IQ has secured a partnership with Kisan America, a established player in the cash recycler industry with existing customer relationships. Under this informal partnership agreement, Kisan America will serve as the distribution and sales channel for Recycler.IQ, bringing the platform to their existing customer base. Five to ten tax authority customers are ready to begin installation upon product release, and one retail customer prospect has expressed intent to procure approximately 200 recycler units, which would require corresponding Recycler.IQ licenses.

**Pricing Model:** The subscription pricing structure is tiered by customer segment:
- Tax Authority customers: $60–75 per recycler per month
- Retail customers: approximately $100 per recycler per month

### 3.2 Legal Mind

Legal Mind is an AI-assisted case law research platform in alpha development, built as a .NET MCP (Model Context Protocol) server. To date, 100 hours of development have been invested at $125/hour, totaling $12,500 in labor, plus $100 in infrastructure costs, for a cumulative development investment of $12,600.

The platform provides legal professionals and researchers with an intelligent interface for case law discovery and analysis, leveraging AI capabilities to accelerate legal research workflows. The primary use case is high-value legal research where time savings and comprehensive citation analysis provide significant professional value.

**Market Opportunity:** Legal Mind is currently in alpha testing with internal users (Lewers Logic LLC partners). The platform is positioned for public launch within four to five months, targeting legal professionals and firms performing case law research at scale.

**Pricing Model:** The pricing structure is tiered as follows:
- **Free Tier:** Core functionality for individual researchers
- **Pro Tier:** $300/month for professional use and enhanced research capabilities
- **Enterprise Tier:** Custom pricing for law firms and institutional users
